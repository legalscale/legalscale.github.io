About Us

Legal Scale provides professional corporate legal services to national and international sophisticated clients. While we service a wide range of clients, we have particularly deep experience in private finance and equity markets. We work directly with our clients to meet their needs and help grow their businesses.

Our Practice Areas

Icon

Venture Equity & Debt

Secure venture capital, including equity issuances, warrants and SAFEs, for seed and growth funding and once companies have achieved product-market fit, obtain venture debt financing.

Icon

Legal Entity Formation

Form separate vehicles to hold assets and/or liabilities, including bankruptcy-remote special purpose entities, for operating companies or funds. Provide operating agreements, resolutions and other formation documents to ensure each vehicle is compliant with existing agreements and regulatory rules.

Icon

Corporate Transactions

Draft agreements, exhibits, schedules, opinions and ancillary documents for a variety of corporate transactions, including mergers and acquisitions, non-disclosure agreements, employment agreements, proprietary information agreements and administrative matters.
